Pixel Mosaic

Overview

This Substance Designer Material takes Pixel Art as an input and creates a Mosaic out of it. In the images shown below, the duck is the Pixel Art. The background was created from scratch in Substance Designer. I added multiple parameters to change the number and height of waves in the background, the position of hte duck, the frequency of cracks and so on.
I wanted the finished version to look like it could be on a wall near a public swimming pool which hasn't been used in a while.
