Shotgun Wedding

Overview

Shotgun Wedding is a game prototype created by me with the help of two friends.
Pixel Art by Nico (@TheTable).
Most of the Envirement was made by @Nikau.

I took care of the Programming, VFX, SFX, Game Design, created the Bridge and Bar scenes, and played an important part in establishing the art direction.

Two of the features I'm most proud of are the particle effects and the custom lighting model.
