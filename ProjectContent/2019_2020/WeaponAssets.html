Desert Environment

Overview

These three Weapons I made to practice an overall Production Workflow.
The Workflow consisted of:
- Getting References
- Creating a Low-Poly first
- Creating a High-Poly out of the Low-Poly
- Creating Materials in Substance Painter or Designer
- Applying Materials and Smart masks in Substance Designer
- Adjusting the masks by hand painting Details.

The first piece I made - the sword Frostmourne - was imported into UE4 to make sure I set up everything correctly.
I made sure everything is up to PBR standards and used a OcclusionRoughnessMetallic Map.


What I learned

Before I created these assets, I had very limited experience in texturing or texture-authoring. That is why I made an absolute beginner mistake on my first piece. For each different Material - Dark Metal, Leather, Emmission, Blue Metal - I made a separate Material. When creating the axes, I made sure to just use one single Material.

I used the process of creating the Assets to learn more about Substance Designer and to get my first experience with substance Designer. It was well worth the effort! Previously I was reluctant to get started with texturing but now I am confident when tackling a full asset production.
