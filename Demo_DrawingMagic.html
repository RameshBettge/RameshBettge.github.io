DrawingMagic Demo

This is just a rough demo and not a complete game!


How to leave the cell:

- Look into pipe to spawn blob
- Stand in front of the door
- Look at the blob first and then at the key
- The blob will fetch the key for you.


About drawing

To unlock the ability to draw you have to get close to the blue box outsite of the cell. Nothing will happen when you draw - currently it is a purely visual effect.
